ABOUT THE HOSPITAL
We at Hospital are dedicated to providing best possible health care at affordable prices. We are a hundred bedded hospital with latest equipment and an I.C.U. We specialise in Medicine, Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Paediatrics, Orthopaedics, ENT, Ophthalmology, Cardiology, Hepatology, Neurology, Urology, Psychiatrics, Dermatology, Physiotherapy, Dentistry and Anaesthesiology. We also have a Pathology department, Major/Minor OT, Post Op Care, Pain Clinic, X-Ray, Ultrasound, Colour Doppler, Echocardiography, E.C.G., Cytology, Endoscopy, EEG, Family Planning Services and a Pharmacy.

"We believe in prevention better than cure."

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.
